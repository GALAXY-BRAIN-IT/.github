# GALAXY MIND AI LABS

Welcome to the official Hugging Face organization for Galaxy Mind AI Labs.  
We are a non-profit startup dedicated to open research in large-scale machine learning, multimodal systems, and agent-based frameworks.

---

## Focus Areas

We explore and develop:
- Large language models (LLMs) and scalable training techniques
- Multimodal architectures combining text, vision, and speech
- Agent frameworks for reasoning, planning, and tool integration
- Evaluation benchmarks for long-context understanding, alignment, and robustness
- Graph learning, knowledge representation, and network analysis

---

## Mission

As a non-profit initiative, our goal is to:
- Advance open-weight AI research
- Provide accessible tools for researchers, developers, and communities
- Promote responsible and transparent use of AI technologies
- Contribute to long-term understanding of intelligence
